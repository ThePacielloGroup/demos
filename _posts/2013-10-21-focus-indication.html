In terms of WCAG AA, there is no prescribed contrast ratio for buttons, however there is a requirement for the focus indication to be 'visible'. We recommend the following.

  • A consistent indication across all focusable elements, including at least an outline style.
  • Outline must be at least 2px thick.
  • Outline must have 3:1 colour contrast against focused element, Or have an outline-offset gap between focused element of at least 1px.
  • Outline must have 3:1 contrast between whatever background it's placed on.

It is important to bear in mind that browsers' default focus indication is often insufficient because it is either too weak to contrast against the background (bear in mind that a dashed outline has significantly less contrast than a solid one).

Also, if you are relying on subtle changes of colour to indicate changes in state for buttons, these may not be visible on certain types of display or in certain environmental/lighting conditions, so either making the colour change more striking, or having an additional (e.g. outline) focus indicator can be of great help.

Please note that any changes to elements that are applied on hover should also be applied on focus.
